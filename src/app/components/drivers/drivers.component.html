How it works

Application

You make an application, to advertise on your vehicle that you drive around a lot.

Wrap

If application is successful, we wrap your vehicle. Coverage area depends on contract. You can decide if you think the ad is suitable, no obligation.

Get Paid

We pay you every month. Amount depends on size and type of vehicle. We can pay you via, bank transfer or PayPal.

Unwrap

At the end of the campaign, we will remove the wrap from your vehicle.
